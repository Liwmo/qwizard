About Qwizard

Qwizard is a web app that allows users to participate in fun, company-related quizzes. The app is being developed by the 2015 Qwizard Summer Intern Team. It works with all modern browsers.

How does it work?

Try to do your best on quizzes to accumulate as many points as possible. Make sure you enter your hours in Ace before 9:00 AM the next day to receive 2 bonus points. Players at the top of the leaderboard for each round of quizzes will have a chance to win a prize!
