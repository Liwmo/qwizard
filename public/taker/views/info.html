About Qwizard

Qwizard is a web app that allows users to participate in fun, company-related quizzes. The app is being developed by the 2015 Qwizard Summer Intern Team. It works with all modern browsers.

How does it work?

Try to do your best on quizzes to accumulate as many points as possible. Scores will be kept weekly and monthly, and there will be prizes awarded based on scores from time to time.  The method for determining who wins a prize will be communicated at the time the prize is announced.

I hear there are prizes!

Prizes based on quiz results will be announced periodically.  The better you do on the quiz, the more likely you are to win!

Who makes the quizzes?

Management and the HR team, but if you have ideas for questions, please send them to hr@asynchrony.com!

One of the questions has the wrong answer!

Our quiz makers aren’t always perfect. If you think you have answered a question correctly, but it was marked incorrect, please report it as a bug. Please make sure you include the quiz and question name.
